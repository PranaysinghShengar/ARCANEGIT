ABOUT US

Mango is a tropical fruit known for its sweet and juicy flesh. Belonging to the genus Mangifera, it is native to South Asia but is now grown in many tropical and subtropical regions worldwide. Mangoes come in various shapes, sizes, and colors, with the most common being oval or oblong and green, yellow, or red when ripe. They are not only delicious but also packed with essential vitamins and minerals, particularly vitamin C and vitamin A, making them a nutritious addition to one's diet. Mangoes can be enjoyed fresh, in smoothies, salads, salsas, or even dried as a tasty snack. Their versatility and delightful flavor have earned them a prominent place in culinary traditions and popular culture across the globe.

VARIETIES

Tommy at Kins Mango

The “Tommy Atkins” mango comes from Florida and is one of the most common mango varieties available to buy.This type of mango is fairly large in size and has an oval shape. The firm yellow flesh is stringy and fibrous.

Alfonso Mango

Alfonso mangoes are a variety of mango originating in India and they have been called “the king of mangoes.” The reason for this description is that Alfonso mangoes are very sweet and juicy.

Neelam Mango

Another large type of Indian variety of mango is the Neelam mango. This mango is so tasty that many people claim it’s the best mango in the world.The juiciest and sweetest Neelam mangoes are available in the middle of summer.

Valencia Pride Mango

Valencia Pride mangoes grow in Florida and California and are an example of a type of large mango fruit.The Valencia Pride cultivar ripens late in the season with the harvest taking place during July and August.

Chaunsa Mango

The Indian mango variety, the Chaunsa mango, is one of the best mango varieties in the world.

Kensington Pride Mango

The Kensington Pride mango, or Bowen mango, is an Australian variety of mango and also the most popular kind of mango in the country.Compared to other types of mango, Kensington Pride mangoes are a big mango variety.
